Recruitment Guidelines

Everything you need to know before applying to join the Man O' War Nigeria.

Eligibility & Application Requirements

  • Age Requirement: Applicant must be a Nigerian citizen aged 18 or above.
  • Health Status: Applicants must be in good physical and mental health.
  • Educational Qualification: Minimum qualification of SSCE or its equivalent.
  • Discipline: You must be ready to uphold the core values of integrity, service, and unity.
  • Documentation: Ensure all information provided is valid and verifiable.

Note:

Falsification of documents or misconduct during recruitment may lead to disqualification or further disciplinary action. Kindly adhere strictly to the guidelines and stay updated via our official communication channels.
