Our National Commander

Meet the leader steering the affairs of Man O' War Nigeria

Prince Adedamola Gbenga Emmanuel

National Commander, Man O' War Nigeria

Prince Adedamola Gbenga Emmanuel is a distinguished leader with over 25 years of service in Man O' War Nigeria. He was appointed as the National Commander in 2023 and has since led the organization with vision and dedication.

Under his leadership, Man O' War Nigeria has expanded its community service initiatives, strengthened partnerships with government agencies, and enhanced youth development programs across all 36 states of the federation.

A recipient of numerous national awards for his contributions to youth development, NC Adedamola Gbenga Emmanuel holds a master in peace and security studies and is widely recognized as an authority on youth mentorship and community development.

"Our mission is to mold Nigerian youths into disciplined, patriotic and responsible citizens who will contribute positively to national development."
